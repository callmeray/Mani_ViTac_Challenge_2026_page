Overview

Building on the 2025 edition, this year's challenge sharpens the focus on robust multi-modal policy learning and reproducible real-robot evaluation.

ManiSkill-ViTac Challenge 2026 is a real-robot challenge on bimanual manipulation with fused visual and tactile sensing in contact-rich tasks. It builds on the ViTaMIn-B visuo-tactile interface, standardizing hardware assumptions and data format into a common benchmark for training and evaluating multi-modal embodied policies.

The 2026 edition currently centers on two tracks: a test-tube manipulation track that combines pick and place with cleaning, and a grinding track oriented toward laboratory-style embodied manipulation. Together they target core capabilities in perception, contact control, bimanual coordination and robust real-world execution.

Real-Robot Finals

Top entries will be validated on a unified hardware setup to measure sim-to-real transfer instead of simulation-only performance.

Visuo-Tactile Benchmarking

Policies must interpret contact-rich signals from both vision and tactile sensing under task variation and object diversity.

Broader Task Coverage

The two-track design covers both dexterous test-tube handling and material-processing style manipulation in lab settings.

Challenge Format

Inspired by more complete challenge sites, the 2026 competition is organized as a two-phase pipeline from open preparation to final real-robot evaluation.

Phase I

Open Preparation and Screening

Feb. to Apr. 2026

Registration, proposal screening, dataset examples, baseline release and early development all happen in this phase.

  • Open team registration and onboarding
  • Dataset examples and documentation release
  • Proposal and offline readiness review
Phase II

Evaluation and Workshop Finals

May to Jun. 2026

Selected teams move into the official evaluation loop, leaderboard updates and final workshop presentation.

  • Real-time evaluation with limited attempts
  • Leaderboard publication and final ranking
  • Workshop award ceremony at CVPR 2026

Tracks

The 2026 edition is organized around two concrete laboratory manipulation tracks.

Track 1

Test Tube Pick and Place + Cleaning

Track 1 focuses on test tube pick-and-place, insertion and cleaning tasks in laboratory settings.

  • Test tube pick and place or insertion tasks
  • Internal tube cleaning with a brush
Track 2

Grinding Operation

Track 2 focuses on grinding tasks with mortar-and-pestle style tools in laboratory settings.

  • Grinding tasks with laboratory tools and materials
  • Mortar-and-pestle style tool use
