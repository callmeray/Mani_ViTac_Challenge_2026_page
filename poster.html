Overview
The 2024 and 2025 editions built a simulation-based sim-to-real pipeline for tactile manipulation. However, many real-world scenarios — transparent objects, liquids, granular materials and fragile solids — remain extremely difficult to simulate. ManiSkill-ViTac Challenge 2026 addresses this gap by providing real-world bimanual demonstration data collected with the ViTaMIn-B2 platform, featuring synchronized wrist cameras and DuoTact visuo-tactile sensors. Participants train language-conditioned visuo-tactile policies directly from these demonstrations and are evaluated on the same physical hardware, advancing research toward TVLA (Tactile-Vision-Language-Action) models — unifying tactile sensing, visual perception and language understanding within a single action policy.
Challenge Tasks
🧪
Task 1 Test Tube Cleaning
Pick up glass test tubes, place them into a rack, and clean the tube interior with a brush. Tactile sensing is critical for compliant grasping of transparent tubes.
  • Test tube pick-and-place and rack insertion
  • Internal cleaning with a brush
  • Generalization across tube sizes and rack configurations
⚗️
Task 2 Grinding
Grind materials in a mortar using a pestle. Bimanual coordination and contact force regulation, with tactile feedback as the primary control signal.
  • Force-regulated grinding with mortar and pestle
  • Bimanual coordination under contact-rich conditions
  • Evaluation on grinding quality and task completion
ViTaMIn-B2 Hardware Platform
Bimanual Visuo-Tactile Data Collection System
All demonstration data is collected using ViTaMIn-B2, an updated bimanual teleoperation system featuring synchronized wrist cameras for visual observation, Meta Quest 3 controllers for 6-DoF bimanual pose acquisition, and DuoTact visuo-tactile sensors on each hand. A foot pedal triggers recording start/end, leaving both hands free during operation.
